Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2025

PetAI Pro takes your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

Information we collect:

  • Pet basic information: name, species, breed, age (for in-app display only)
  • Usage statistics: sound recognition count, photo analysis count (local storage only)
  • Subscription status: whether subscribed, subscription type (verified through Apple servers)

Information we do NOT collect:

  • Audio data: All sound recognition is completed locally on the device and will not be uploaded
  • Image data: All visual analysis is completed locally on the device and will not be uploaded
  • Location information: We do not access your geographic location
  • Device identifiers: We do not collect device unique identifiers

2. Information Usage

  • Pet information is only used for in-app display and personalized experience
  • Usage statistics are used to display today's stats and limit free user usage
  • Subscription status is used to control feature access permissions
  • We will not use your information for advertising
  • We will not sell or share your information with third parties

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is only stored locally on your device (UserDefaults and file system)
  • We do not use cloud servers to store your data
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all local data
  • We use Apple-provided standard security mechanisms to protect data

4. Subscription and Payment

  • Subscription purchases are processed through Apple App Store
  • We do not store your credit card or payment information
  • We only receive subscription status notifications provided by Apple
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in iOS Settings

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

Email: support@petai.app
